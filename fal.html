Far Above Logic

Far above logic
Love they say magic
Day today hectic
What I write poetic

Tea in my kettle
All I know little
News but all tattle
Youths are tagged nettle

Back in that era
Dad we called teacher
Maths we learned better
Base we know bitter

Now in this nation
Words without action
Price in high motion
Peace is now fiction

How I feel blest
When I do my best
Pain I put to rest
Red my hands arrest
