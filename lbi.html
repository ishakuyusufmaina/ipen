Love Beyond Infinity

Let's play like that time a game
At the end we all won the same
My love for you nothing but a rain
In ocean no mortal can drain

With you I will grow a village
With no means to break the lineage
That will be set on the map
At the time we took our nap

Your life to mine is a culture
That never will know contracture
Safe I assured you in my arm
Mortal has no power to harm

I preferred you to that degree
To lose them but have you agree
Mine I can sell yours to be bought
That I can with no second thought

When they ask for understatement
Maths I use to give the measurement
Just infinity your love I rate
On all measures it has no mate
