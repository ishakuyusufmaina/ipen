At Park Or Paradise

In science or art
In commerce or government
I will reveal the fact
You are what I found

At home or school
Today or tomorrow
I will pay you a visit
To prove I love

On Earth or cloud
At park or paradise
With you I will selfie
To prove togetherness

In light or darkness
In pain or happiness
I will hold your hand
To disgrace your enemies
