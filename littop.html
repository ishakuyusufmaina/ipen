Love in the Time of Physics

The Negative charge
To my Positive charge
And South pole
To my North pole
Let my words
Flow Like a river
From me to you
Transfer Like a heat
From hot to cold

Your anger
Stirs mine
Random motion

Seeing you at a distance
Makes me move
At an angle of 180Â°
And renders my gait
Acceleration

The melody of your voice
Asks me dance
Simple harmonic motion

When I see you
I walk up to you
In a dynamic equilibrium
Fix my eyes on you
Like a static equilibrium

If temperature
Is degree of hotness
Your smile
Is degree of happiness

Your presence
Makes me feel composed
Like two waves
Which are exactly out of phase

Action and reaction
Are equal
My love for you and rain
Are equal

Let us fly
Like a projectile
Against the gravitational pull
To visit the rotational moon
With the use of our kinetic energy
And come over the earth
With the use of our potential energy

Why won't we lose
Our molecular
kinetic energy
To become solidified
For you to be my singularity
And for me to be
Your solidarity?

With you
As a direction
I feel like a vector
Without you
I am just a scalar

I promise you
In my heart
Your love won't decay
It will live
With a power of half-life
