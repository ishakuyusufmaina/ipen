Page To Page

Page to page
Town to town
People kidnapped
Even are killed
Some are burnt
Skirts are raped
Oh my God!
How I wish
Those enthroned
Knock at them
What we feel
Day by day
Price increased
Way to farm
Access denied
On all metrics
People are ebb
What we did
To deserve the slap
By those we vote
To assume the throne?
When we say
Enough is enough!
Change the change!
Friends they fight
Enemy impressed
Brain in cage!
